Gourmet Meat Curry

A warming stew that is sure to stick to your ribs!

Combining the finest of cuts of meat with spicy and delicious Goron spice blend, this curry will wake you up with it's vibrant flavor palette. Note that while there is no substitute for the real thing, Ethiopian Berbere would make an acceptable substitute for the Goron Spice Blend.

Ingredients

  • 3 flanks of Tabantha moose meat
  • 1 bundle of Hylian rice
  • 1 bottle of Goron spice powder

Preparation

  1. Mill the rice
  2. Saute the spice powder in oil until fragrant
  3. Add meat to sauteed spice blend and cook until the meat develops a light crust.
  4. Add 3 cups water or stock and allow to simmer until meat is tender and falls apart easily, about 4-5 hours.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
