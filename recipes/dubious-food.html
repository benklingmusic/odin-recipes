Dubious Food

It isn't much but it's food

Look, we've all been there. You open the fridge and just stare into the void until the fridge beeps at you for leaving the door open for too long, breaking your trance and bringing you back to the land of the living. You need to eat. It's important. But everything requires work and you're tired. Whatever, just throw together what you can and call it a day. How bad could it be right?

Ingredients

  • 1 Lizalfo tail
  • 1 horn of Bokoblin
  • 1 Endura carrot
  • 1 bunch of mighty bananas

Preparation

  1. Combine ingredients in pot over open fire.
  2. Jam out to this. sick. beat.
  3. Be disappointed with the result but eat it anyway.
